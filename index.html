Pandora Style Guide

Logo Typography Colors Symbols Mobile

Logo

The logo can be paired with the name of the company. The color should not be changed, nor should the logo be stretched in any way.

Typography

Pandora uses Avinir Next as their font, an easy to read yet modern sans serif.

Header title - 400/36/48

Subheading title - 400/24/36

Body - 400/18/28

Bold body - 700/15/20.5

Colors

Pandora's color scheme primarily consists of blue colors, although other colors may be used, particularly with the monogram. These colors should be bold, however.

Since this is a music servicce, Pandora's focus lays in a clean, fun use of their buttons. Icons are used for the music player while buttons are used for text such as log in and signup.
